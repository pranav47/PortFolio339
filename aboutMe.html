WHO IS THIS GUY?

Hi, My name is Pranav Sharma and I am design student based in Vancouver. I enjoy design in all media, but I am a big fan of visual and product design. A quote from the famous Industrial designer Dieter Rams “Less but better” really influenced me as a designer, and whenever I design I like to keep my designs functional and true to their purpose. I enjoy UI design roles and I am currently interested in positions in that domain.
